Nice Slice was established in 2005 and have been doing it every day since. By "it" We mean opening at 11 am and staying open until 12am in the morning to serve the best pizza you ever put your lips on. It has been rough at times but we have overcome the obstacles and are here to stay. This is because we love pizza. It is the center of the universe and drives everything creative and wonderful. It brings people together and has mysterious power scientists have yet to fully comprehend...Come and have a slice to experience it for yourself...
