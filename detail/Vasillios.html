Vasilios pizza has been proudly serving Providence R.I for 8 years. Our daily fresh made Greek dough and pizza sauce is a masterd, delicous special recipe brought from Greece over 52 years ago! Keeping to that tradition, our pizza features a tasty crunchy crust along with our flavorful, well seasoned pizza sauce and the finest combination of our melty chedar and mozzerla cheese.
