Napolitano's prides itself on being one of the only true New York pizzerias in the state, offering whole 18" pies as well as slices, calzones, strombolis and more. The Naploitano staff works hard everyday producing all of our menu items fresh. The menu is inspired by tradition, offering old family recipes as well as some new additions.
