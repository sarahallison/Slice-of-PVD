Sicilia’s of Federal Hill frozen deep-dish pizza comes from one of America’s greatest Italian neighborhoods. This specialty pizza has been a Federal Hill favorite for over 30 years. Each pie is handcrafted using the best ingredients: rolled, hand-tossed dough; generous handfuls of fresh mozzarella; and Sicilia’s fresh, specially made tomato sauce.The result is a hearty, classic Italian meal that features a soft cheesy center, a tangy savory marinara sauce and a light, crunchy crust that alone or with toppings creates a flavorful and filling meal that will satisfy even the heartiest appetite. Enjoy!﻿
